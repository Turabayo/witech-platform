About Us

WiTech is a project to tackle the gender disparities prevalent in the technology industry by providing a multifaceted solution that empowers women to excel in this field.

Services

Key Purpose.

Education

The primary purpose is to offer accessible and quality education to women, equipping them with the technical skills and knowledge necessary to thrive in the tech industry.

Mentorship and Networking

Another critical purpose is to facilitate mentorship and networking opportunities. This involves connecting experienced women in technology with newcomers, fostering a sense of community, and providing guidance for career advancement.

Advocacy and Awareness

The project aspires to raise awareness about the gender disparities that persist in the tech sector and advocate for gender-inclusive policies and practices within tech companies.

Career Advancement

A central purpose is to enhance career prospects for women in tech. This includes offering career services such as job listings, internship opportunities, and tools for resume building and interview preparation.
